Jeeyar Gurukulam, Beersaipet


  • In continuation with the Allampalli project, a Jeeyar Gurukulam school was established at Beersaipeta in Utnur Mandal of Adilabad District. Utnur is a nearby small village which is about 12 km away. The school began with 108 students during 2005 in thatched huts.
  • With the help of philanthropists, a school building was constructed and inaugurated on 27 July 2007 with science lab, computer lab, library and spacious classrooms.
  • Free Food, Education, Study Material, Clothing and Health Care are provided for all these children. 302 students are studying in this gurukulam as of Apr 2017. Read More....

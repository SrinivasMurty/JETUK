Nethra Vidyalaya

EDUCATION FOR BLIND

Nethra Vidyalaya provides education from primary level to high school, junior & senior college, where hundreds of blind students are benefiting every year.

Nethra Vidyalaya is unique in that students are trained to write their Intermediate board exams and Degree exams on laptops without the help of scribes (helpers). This education system is the first and unique in India where the state government recognized and permitted the students to write their exams on laptops.



JET provides FREE boarding, lodging, food, study material, education for the blind etc.., to all communities irrespective of religion, caste, colour and gender.

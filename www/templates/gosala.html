PROTECT MOTHER EARTH.

Since 1984, 400+ cows have been taken cared for at 3 gosalas in different locations.

    Our goals are
  • Saving the indigenous breeds
  • Protect the old and neglected cows
  • Encourage the use of organic products (Deerghayuvu)
  • Spreading importance of cow & cow products

    Benefits of Cow & Cow Products:
  • Cow urine and dung increases the soil fertility
  • Cow based products increases lifespan & promote good health
  • Industries are using panchagavyas (Milk, Yoghurt, Ghee, Cow urine, Cow dung) for manufacturing cosmetic products.
  • Cow plays a big role in pollution reduction & environment protection

    Our cow products:
  • Deerghayuvu Go:rasam - Best medicine for many diseases
  • Deerghayuvu Shampoo - Removes hair fall and dandruff
  • Deerghayuvu Toothpowder - Cures gum swelling, toothache, and tooth decay
  • Deerghayuvu Charma Rakshaka - Prevents allergies and rashes
  • Deerghayuvu Facepack - Cures pimples, spots and conditions the skin

    You can protect by doing any of the following:
  • Go:pu:ja
  • Go:da:nam
  • Donate Green grass (Go:grassam)
  • Cover medical expenses

Go:puja means worshipping Go:ma:tha (cow) along with her calf. Go:puja fulfills the wishes and removes hardships of the devotee who performs it.

Go:da:nam means donating a cow (Go:ma:tha) along with her calf. Go:da:nam liberates the donor and his forefathers from the sins committed knowingly or unknowingly in their lives. It bestows heavenly comforts on the donor.

    Get involved in our Gosala by:
  • Feeding the cows
  • Cleaning them
  • Milking them
  • Health Care
